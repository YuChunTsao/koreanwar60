America Commemorates the 60th Anniversary of the Korean War Armistice

July 27, 2013 - 8:00 AM
Korean War Veterans Memorial Washington, DC

Make plans to attend a special ceremony observing the 60th Anniversary of the Korean War Armistice presented by the Department of Defense. This special salute will include tributes to Korean War Veterans, recognition of UN Allies, and formal remarks from Senior Government Officials.
