America Commemorates the 60th Anniversary of the Korean War Armistice

July 27, 2013 - 8:00 AM

Korean War Veterans Memorial
Washington, DC

Make plans to attend a special ceremony observing the 60th Anniversary of the Korean War Armistice presented by the Department of Defense. This special salute will include tributes to Korean War Veterans, recognition of UN Allies, and formal remarks from Senior Government Officials.

Pentagon Parking Opens6:00 AM

Important Note

COME EARLY! Attendees arriving after 8:45 AM will be directed to the general admission site.

Event Highlights

8:00 AM, Program Begins

  • Special tributes to Korean War Veterans
  • Video presentations
  • Korean American cultural presentations
  • Ceremonial wreath layings
  • Simulcast from Korea marking the 60th anniversary of when the guns went silent
  • Formal remarks from Senior Government Officials
